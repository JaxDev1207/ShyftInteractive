Shyft Interactive (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting the privacy and security of users (“you,” “your”). This policy applies to our official website (shyftinteractive.com), all games and applications developed by Shyft Interactive, and related networked or social features integrated via third-party platforms. It does not govern third-party websites or services outside our control.

3. Information We Collect

(a) Account Information: Display name, username, PlayFab ID, and platform-specific identifiers.

(b) Gameplay & Network Data: In-game interactions, scores, progress, session logs, matchmaking and connection details processed for multiplayer functionality.

We do not knowingly collect sensitive personal information such as financial credentials or government identifiers.

We process information to provide, operate, and improve the Services, to enable multiplayer connectivity via Photon or Mirror VR, to store player data via PlayFab, to improve gameplay and performance, and to enforce our Terms of Service and community guidelines.
